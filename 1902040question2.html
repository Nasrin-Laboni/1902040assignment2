Welcome. Police Lines School & College, Rangpur

Like all other educational institutions in our country, Police Lines School and College, Rangpur had a small beginning. With a view to spreading quality education among the pedigree of the police personnel serving in this part of the country, as well as the children of the socially, educationally and economically backward people of the greater Rangpur area, the institution was founded in 1986 and since then it has been striving to the pursuit of excellence combining quality education and disciplined academic environment.
